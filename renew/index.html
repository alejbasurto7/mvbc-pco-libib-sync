Managing Your Libib Account

A guide to your MVBC Library account

On this page

  • I. Create a Libib account
  • II. Renew a checkout
  • III. Update your password
Quick renewal shortcut: sign in at libib.com/u/mvbchurch, open the Active tab, and click Renew next to the book. The walkthrough below covers it step-by-step with screenshots if you'd like to follow along.

I.Create a Libib account

  1. Visit MVBC's Libib website at libib.com/u/mvbchurch.
  2. Click the Patron Login option in the top-right corner.
  3. In the Patron Login dialog, click the Need Password? link.
  4. Enter the email address you use to check out books and click Submit.
  5. Copy the password from the email Libib sends to that address. (You can replace it with one you'll remember — see Section III.)
  6. Go back to libib.com/u/mvbchurch and click Patron Login again.
  7. Enter your email address and paste the password from the Libib email, then click Login.

II.Renew a checkout

  1. Sign in to your Patron account (see Section I, steps 1–2 and 7).
  2. Click the Active option in the menu bar.
  3. Find the book you want to renew and click its Renew button.
  4. Confirm by clicking Renew in the dialog.
  5. Verify your new due date.

III.Update your password

  1. Sign in to your Patron account (see Section I).
  2. Click the Edit Profile option in the menu bar.
  3. Scroll down to the Password section.
  4. Enter your New Password, its Confirmation, and your Current Password. Click Change Password.
