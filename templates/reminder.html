How to check out a book

  1. On the iPad, scan the barcode of each book you'd like to check out. (The camera is on the back of the iPad.)
  2. Tap the Checkout button.
  3. Enter your email ({email}) or scan your library card, then tap OK.

You have 3 weeks to return each book. Drop returns into one of the boxes by the kiosk.
