  Save it to your home screen — one tap forever after:
    - iPhone: Open the link in Safari (not Gmail's in-app browser).
      Tap the Share button (square with an up-arrow), scroll the share sheet
      down, and tap "Add to Home Screen".
    - Android: Open the link in Chrome. Tap the ⋮ menu (top-right) and
      tap "Add to Home screen" (or "Install app").

  Tip: if you tapped this link from inside the Gmail app on iPhone, choose
  "Open in Safari" first — Gmail's built-in browser hides the Share menu.
