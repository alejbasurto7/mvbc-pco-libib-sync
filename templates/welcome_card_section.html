Save it to your home screen — one tap forever after:

  • iPhone: Open the link in Safari (not Gmail's in-app browser). Tap the Share button (square with an up-arrow) at the bottom, scroll the share sheet down, and tap Add to Home Screen.
  • Android: Open the link in Chrome. Tap the ⋮ menu (top-right) and tap Add to Home screen (or Install app).

Tip: if you opened this email in the Gmail app on iPhone, tap the link, then on the page that opens tap the ··· menu and choose Open in Safari first — Gmail's built-in browser hides the Share menu.
