HOW TO USE YOUR NEW CARD

You already know the drill — scan your books at the kiosk and tap
'Checkout'. The only change is at the final step:

  - Instead of typing your email ({email}), just scan your library
    card with the iPad camera and tap 'OK'.
  - Your email still works as a backup if you don't have your card
    on you.

OUR LIBRARIES
Both libraries share the same room, right next to the Fellowship Hall.

  • MVBC Library — Commentaries, Christian Living, Biblical Theology,
    Family and Parenting, Marriage and Relationships, Biographies,
    Church History, and more.
    https://www.libib.com/u/mvbchurch/l/812861

  • Children's Library — Children's bibles, picture books, early readers,
    and chapter books for kids.
    https://www.libib.com/u/mvbchurch/l/471546

Same as always: 3 weeks per book, drop returns in the boxes by the
kiosk, and self-renew through the catalog (sign in -> Active -> Renew)
if you need more time — walkthrough:
  https://alejbasurto7.github.io/mvbc-pco-libib-sync/renew/
